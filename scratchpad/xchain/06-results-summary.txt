Verdict
-------
FAILED — the hypothesis is not supported.

The binary flow confirmation gate applied daily causes extreme position churn.
Daily TVL percentage changes oscillate between positive and negative frequently,
so the 0.5x floor triggers position exits and the 1.5x boost triggers re-entries
on a near-daily basis. This destroyed position stability without improving returns.

CAGR fell by 1.52 pp, Sortino fell by 0.93, and position count exploded from 18
to 922 with 79.8% of positions lasting fewer than 3 days.

The flow confirmation concept needs smoothing (longer window, threshold band,
or EMA-based signal) to avoid reacting to daily TVL measurement noise.
