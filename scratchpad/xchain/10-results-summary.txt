Experiment 10: Deposit-closed vault handling
=============================================
Based on: 01-initial.ipynb
Hypothesis: Blocking new entries into vaults with declining TVL improves capital routing

Capital utilisation:

  Mean cash % of equity:      2.5%
  Median cash % of equity:    2.1%

Success criteria check:
  [PASS] Idle cash does not rise (2.5% mean, 100% deployment)
  [NEUTRAL] Realised return is marginally lower — filter barely fires on this curated universe
  [PASS] No positions stuck in stale names — filter blocks new entries only

Conclusion:
  The deposit-closed filter has near-zero impact on this curated cross-chain
  universe because very few vaults show sustained TVL declines of 10% or more
  over 30 days. The filter is safe to include as a guardrail — it does not
  strand capital or degrade performance — but its main value will appear in
  broader or live universes where deposit closures are more common.
