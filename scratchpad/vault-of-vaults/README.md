# Vault of vaults - experiment notebooks

This folder contains a progressive series of Jupyter notebooks exploring allocation strategies for a "vault of vaults" portfolio — a meta-strategy that invests across multiple DeFi yield vaults (primarily on Hyperliquid, Arbitrum, and other chains).

The series evolves from hand-picked vault prototypes (NB01–NB09) through cross-chain diversification (NB10–NB25), systematic parameter search (NB26–NB45), Calmar signal engineering (NB46–NB53), probabilistic allocation methods (NB54–NB65), dual-signal architectures (NB66–NB70), rank composite metadata selection (NB71), cash deployment optimisation with HLP absorption and dispersion-aware weighting (NB72–NB73), gate variant and universe boundary tuning (NB74–NB76), cross-chain dual-signal integration (NB77), robustness validation with extended history testing (NB78–NB80), walk-forward validation and gate/signal ablation studies (NB81–NB86), wide universe signal exploration (NB87–NB88), share-price and structural signal testing (NB89–NB95), and a comprehensive age-ramp audit with 18 experiments decomposing why age-based weighting beats equal weight (NB96–NB113).
